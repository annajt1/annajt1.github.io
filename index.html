English Tuition


Online tuition in English and English Literature for students at KS3, GCSE and A Level. 1:1 lessons



If your child needs some support with literacy, I can help them reach their potential. I specialize in both English Language and Literature, having over 30 years classroom experience teaching KS3, GCSE and A Level.

I have also been a DSL for many years and hold an enhanced DBS.

I have had recent experience with a variety of exam boards-AQA, Edexcel and WJEC.

I offer bespoke 1:1 lessons online and face to face. Small group online lessons are also available. Please feel free to contact me for a chat about your child's needs.
